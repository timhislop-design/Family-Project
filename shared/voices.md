# Voices She Loves

The writers Abby respects, and what she loves about each — in **her own words**. This is a mirror, not a map: naming what moves her in other writers helps her notice what she's drawn to, and that's how her own voice shows up.

**The rule:** influences guide *technique and feel* — the rhythm, the humor, how a story opens, how characters talk. We never copy an author's actual words. We learn the *move*, and she makes it hers.

**How to add one:** say *"add a voice"* and tell me the author and what she likes about them. Or fill in a card below together.
