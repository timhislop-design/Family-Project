# Our Family Book Project

This is where the three of us — Dad, Abby, and Claude (our writing helper) — make books together.

## Who does what

- **Abby — the ideas.** The brilliant part. Characters, worlds, what-ifs, feelings. The story is hers and starts with her words.
- **Dad — the editor.** Shaping, trimming, asking "is this the truest version?", protecting her voice, deciding what's ready. Editing *is* creating — it's where a good idea becomes a real book.
- **Claude — the in-between.** I capture her words fast so nothing's lost, draft and re-draft on request, keep everything organized and separate, and hand you clean material to edit. I do the busywork so you two can do the magic.

The flow: **Abby dreams it → Claude captures and shapes a draft → Dad edits it true → Abby decides if it's right.** She always has the last word on her own story.

Every idea gets its own space so nothing gets mixed up. You can have a hundred ideas going at once and they'll never bump into each other unless we decide to bring them together.

## How it works

1. **You have an idea.** A character, a world, a story, a feeling — anything.
2. **We start a project for it.** Each idea gets its own folder inside `projects/`. It's all yours and stays separate.
3. **Your words come first.** Every project starts mostly empty. We write down *your* ideas and *your* words first. Claude only helps shape and grow what you give — the story stays yours.
4. **Stray ideas have a home too.** If you think of something that doesn't fit yet, it goes in that project's `ideas-parking-lot.md` so we never lose it.
5. **Sharing is a choice.** Nothing crosses from one book into another unless we *both* decide to move it into the `shared/` folder.

```
  shared/
    characters.md        ← only the things we both agree to share across books
```

## Two magic phrases

You only need to remember two things (full details in `KICKOFF.md`):

- **"New book idea"** → Claude makes a fresh, separate folder and we begin.
- **"Let's edit [name]"** → Claude opens an older project to change the premise, characters, or story.

Say **"show me our projects"** anytime to see the full list in `projects/INDEX.md`.

For voice-building: **"add a voice"** (log an author she loves) or **"voice session"** (notice what's becoming hers). See `shared/voices.md`.
