## ✓ DECIDED (Tim & Abby, Jul 10, 2026) — the three forks

**1. Do humans know about the deep? → The rumor tier.** Nobody *knows* — but the surface hums with it. Sightings, encounter stories, grainy sonar clips, the fisherman nobody believes twice. It reads like today's news cycle about things surfacing from the deep: theories everywhere, believers dismissed, and the evidence *mounting* — because the Pelagics have grown careless. Every leak is a scandal below and fuels the strife between the factions. Full canon in `brief.md`.

**2. Government of the deep → elders + the two factions, no royalty.** As canon. Why the factions have existed as long as they have is a held-back reveal — **Book 1 stays light**, and each book darkens as more of that reason surfaces. Tone ladder in `arc.md`.

**3. Prophecy → never formal.** Rumor and superstition only — the deep's own version of the surface's cryptid rumors. Same mechanism, mirrored: both worlds run on stories nobody can prove and everybody repeats. Already in `characters/nerai.md`.

## Abby's questions — with what canon already answers

- **What's politics/society/culture like in the deep?** Partly answered: old, cold-watered, doesn't coddle its young, children are *expected* not embraced, elders + two factions, grown cities, song-speech, long memory, the law of *do not interrupt*. Open: daily life, art, food, festivals, what they owe each other. Also open: Abby's monarchy/royal-council question (parking lot).
- **What does the academy teach?** OPEN. Canon says it's a school for the gifted (fire-handed, water-callers, wind-listeners, shadow) that teaches them to pass for human. Curriculum undefined — good building session.
- **Real-culture inspiration?** Adopted direction: Greek/Mediterranean + Sama-Bajau mix (parking lot).
- **What vibe — high/low/dark fantasy?** Mostly answered: YA, "wonder + tension," clean page, edge through behavior not content. Open: *how dark is the darkest chapter allowed to get?* Worth deciding together — Abby raised The Poppy War as the far end.
- **What's the surface world like — advanced, corrupt?** OPEN. Basically ours-plus-hidden-magic so far. Undefined beyond that.
- **Is Nerai a fan of the surface?** Answered: she's in love with it — the forbidden-longing kind. Collected sunk things as a kid. Her people survive by being unseen; she survives by needing to see.
- **What do magic users draw from? What's the price?** OPEN and important. Rule already on file: magic always costs. The system itself (deep song + surface elements, and what each costs) is unbuilt. Big session.
- **Main theme / what the reader carries out?** Answered: **refusing the inherited story** — and the cost of being right. (See `arc.md` — the narration and the hero share the same instinct.)
- **MC's biggest fear — make it come true.** Adopted as a craft rule (`WRITING-LESSONS.md` #8). Open: name the fear precisely. Current best candidate from canon: *that she was sent away, not sent out — that the people who love her feared her more.* Worth saying out loud together and then aiming the plot at it.
