## Questions to answer (can hopefully help give inspo):

* What are politics/society/culture/etc. like in the deep? And vice versa for the surface?

* What does the academy teach? Could it be just normal subjects? Or is there something unique to this world? Magical in nature?

* Could this world contain any reference or inspiration from real life cultures or mythologies and if so, what? Greek mythology and the Mediterranean cultures, for example, work well with an ocean setting. Maybe draw some inspiration there? IMO mixing real life cultures is the best way to get a unique setting.

* Indonesia is also very good for this kind of setting, specifically could draw inspo from the Sama-Bajau people. Avatar I think drew inspo for the water clans from this.

* A good question to answer is what kind of vibe do you want? Is it high fantasy, lighter, more magical? Or low fantasy, more grounded and realistic? Or is it dark fantasy, darker, grittier, even violent? Fully depends on what you're going for. Also think about the genre, because if it's like young adult fantasy typically it's lighter in tone. Especially since MC is young. Although there are some really good darker books with young MCs like The Poppy War.

* What kind of world do the newer humans live in? Is it more advanced, less? Is it more corrupt?

* Do both societies know of one another or do only the OG people know of the surface people? If so, how have they remained hidden? And how to they keep track of the newer people? Spies? Could MC be one? She was mostly sent for a better life, a safer life, but also to gain info? To save her people?

* It's likely they do know of one another, meaning they most likely communicate in some way, how? Do they trade valuable resources only available to each, they'd likely trade a lot of things. Would they send people up to deal with this business?

* Are they at war?

* Do some OGs like the opportunity on the surface, it's likely some would've integrated, working various jobs on the surface if so. Are they viewed with skepticism, negativity, or are they revered, worshipped by some, maybe even feared?

* Is MC a fan of the surface or does she dislike it and the people who live there? Is she fascinated with them or does she just tolerate them? She feels out of place regardless, but what are her opinions on them?

* Magic is interesting, adding it to the surface people and even the OGs is good but what do they draw from? In a good magic system magic always has a price.

* Overall, what is the main theme being conveyed? What is the message you want the reader to carry with them after reading it?

* What is the MCs biggest fear? Make it come true.
