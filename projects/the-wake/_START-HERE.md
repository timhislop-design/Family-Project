# The Wake — Start Here

**Founded:** July 24, 2026 (Tim & Claude)
**What it is:** The third book in the Family Project — and the first one run as an experiment.

## The two books

This project is actually TWO books:

1. **The Wake** — a sci-fi novel. Premise in `brief.md`. Short version: the solar system is a healed wound from something that passed through before anything could remember it, and the only instrument that ever measured the truth is a keeper family's hundred and forty years of "lighthouse superstition" — logs about to be thrown away because automation is replacing the keepers.

2. **The Second Seat** (working title — Tim can veto) — a companion book about HOW we wrote The Wake. Not a making-of afterthought: a real book about what it's like when a human and an AI build something together with the roles deliberately reversed. Raw material accumulates in `flight-log.md` as we go; the book gets written from the log later. Tim's reason for it, in his words: *"Too much misunderstanding in AI, we can use this to help people understand."*

## The experiment

The Deep and The Firmament started from Tim's and Abby's ideas, with Claude structuring and drafting. This one reverses the seats: **Claude generates and commits; Tim guides, vetoes, and teaches taste.** Full rules in `experiment.md`. The learning itself is on paper — `taste-model.md` is Claude's working model of what Tim (and readers generally) respond to, updated every time Tim reacts, with evidence attached. That legibility is the point: you can watch the collaboration think.

## The three axes (why this book is not the other two)

- The Deep: *the world is bigger than you knew* (discovery fantasy)
- The Firmament: *the world is smaller than you knew* (imprisonment thriller)
- The Wake: *the world is older than you knew* (deep-time mystery)

## File map

- `brief.md` — the committed premise + current attack vectors
- `experiment.md` — rules of the role-reversal experiment
- `flight-log.md` — contemporaneous decision log (raw material for The Second Seat; never retconned)
- `taste-model.md` — Claude's falsifiable hypotheses about taste, with evidence
- `ideas-parking-lot.md` — anything good that doesn't belong yet
- `drafts/` — dated version folders, same convention as the-deep
