# Ideas Parking Lot — The Wake

Good things that don't belong yet. Nothing here is canon.

- **The unclaimed beacons** (Claude, Jul 24): a variant where a few nodes in the relay chain were already there — nobody built them, the families just kept them. Parked because it may over-egg the premise: the wake's evidence should stay scattered and mundane. Revisit only if the middle of the book needs a second engine.
- **The four unpicked premises from the menu round** (Jul 24): The Anchor (the fixed point), The Interest (relativity as class system / inherited debt), The Decommissioners (colony undertakers + the warm greenhouse), The Lamplighters standalone. Any of these could be a future sub-project or a donor organ. The Lamplighters already donated its keeper families to The Wake.
- **Episodic chapter shape** (from the Lamplighters pitch): each chapter a complete small story of something passing through the beacon's light. Might survive into The Wake's structure — good for a project that has to fit around real life.
