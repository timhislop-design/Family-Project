# Parking Lot — THE FIRMAMENT

A place to keep ideas that don't fit *yet*. Nothing is too small or too silly.

- **Title: THE FIRMAMENT — locked (Tim, Jul 23).** Alternates (Vault of Heaven / Under Heaven / Windows of Heaven / Amen) considered and rejected as cheesy. Abby can still react or beat it, but there's no shortlist — beat it outright or it stands.
- **Name shortlists** (all placeholders until Abby's naming pass; keep the meaning even if the name changes): Selah (the lost Psalms word) · brother **Amos** ("carried") · Returned man candidates: Enosh ("mortal man"), Ezra, or a title-only ("the Returned") · high priest title candidates: the First Canter, the Keeper of the Forms.
- **Festival calendar** (mirror The Deep's one-per-act trick, different holidays): **the Opening of the Windows** (first-rain festival — the whole town sings the request together, umbrellas as celebration) · **the Raising** (selection ceremony, the anti-reaping — name WIP) · a night festival for the wandering stars (kids stay up to count angels; lovers name them). One per act, and the Act III one should go publicly wrong.
- **The mothers of the Taken** — a social class of their own: veiled? honored seating? her mother's whole identity. Rich vein for the town's texture and Selah's pressure.
- **Canter guild texture** — how the forms are drilled (call-and-response, error = start over from the top), the older canter who trained her, the rival kid who's *fluent but doesn't listen*.
- **Answered-prayer catalog** — small warm miracles for Act I: the fever prayer, the lost-thing prayer, the travel blessing at the rim road. Each one secretly a diagnostic/command; we never say so.
- **The second channel** (unused protagonist-gift option): under the Voice's public words, a quieter layer like machinery breathing. Rejected as her gift (born-special = formula) but could be something she LEARNS to hear late in Book 1 — earned, not given.
- **Babel, parked**: the pre-Babel "one language" / shattered-tongue premise (the hunted-girl open-world version). Strong enough to be its own book someday; do not fold into this one — one command-language idea per book.
- **Sister-vault story seeds** (upper cosmology, Book 3+/spinoffs — details sealed in canon-vault.md): the silent vault (nothing has answered in centuries — what does faith become?); the early-opened vault; the vault where canters became kings; the deviant-Voice vault. Each one is "same seed text, different drift" — a whole book per dome if we ever want them.
- **The rhyme (touch never)**: Days of Smoke ↔ The Deep's Shattering — see canon-vault.md. A note, not a plan.
- **Screen kit** (poster images to protect in the prose): dome dawn; the recitation where the Voice asks back; weeping proud mothers at the Raising; a wandering star that stops mid-sky; the last shot of Book 1.
- **Grandmother thread** (unused wound option): a hidden older copy of the scripture with margins marked — could become a Book 2 discovery (someone before Selah found the seam and was handled quietly).
