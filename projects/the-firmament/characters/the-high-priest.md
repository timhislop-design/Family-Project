# The High Priest
*(Title TBD — candidates: the First Canter, the Keeper of the Forms. Name Abby's if she wants him to have one.)*

## Who he is
The head of the guild and the voice of the doctrine — and **not a cartoon** (hard rule). He is genuinely learned, genuinely devout, and carries something the institution hands down without understanding: old protocols for *when the Voice deviates*. He has seen a deviation once before in his life. He has never told anyone.

## The opening beat (canon)
At the year's highest ritual, when Selah's letter-perfect old-form recitation makes the Voice do the impossible — answer with something extra, *ask something back* — there is a half-second before he recovers and improvises. In that half-second Selah sees his face, and understands the most dangerous fact in Act I: **someone already knew it could do that.**

## His shape in the book
Act I: authority. Act II: surveillance — he is watching Selah, and she can't tell if it's suspicion or recognition. Act III: the reveal of what he actually knows and what it has cost him to carry it. Obstacle, casualty, or unexpected ally — deliberately unresolved. He should be scarier when he's kind than when he's stern.
